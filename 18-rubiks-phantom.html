Rubik's Phantom

6064627 (2022)

Rubik's Phantom. The color tiles on this cube have a thermochromic element that is black when cold and reveals the color when warmed (presumably while handling). Practically, though, most times here in Guam (especially with no AC running) it's just a regular Rubik's Cube (swipe right), though with colors that are a bit muted. I had to place it in the freezer for a few minutes to get it to be completely black.

That said, it's not exactly like the standard Rubik's Cube, either, as the corner cutting is much better and I feel like I can handle this like a speed cube. It is very loud when being turned, however.

All in all a cool cube for the collection that is different enough from my other Rubik's Cubes (the Speed and the UD "touch" cube). Thanks @stef_zakia_mahlone for the Christmas gift!
