As of this latest update, I have been able to make pages for 11 out of 63 cubes in my collection. These are also all the cubes from YJ I have made previous posts about on my Instagram.

In my haste to catalog all the cubes in my collection, I may have inadvertently included some articles that are strictly not 3×3 "mofang" (magic cubes). These include the Rubik's Slide, some 2×2 and 4×4 cubes, and some infinity cubes that I was given as a gift. These are fun but would not fit into this collection.
