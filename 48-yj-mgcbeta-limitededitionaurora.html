YJ MGC 3 Beta Limited Edition Aurora

YJ8124/Aurora (2025)

YJ MGC 3 Beta, Limited Edition Aurora.

I usually do not get special edition cubes. One popular way cube companies make limited editions is to make them clear. But even though I have quite a predilection for #clearcubes, special edition cubes have been over my spending limit (which is $50). So in my mind, when I discovered that the price of this item was $29.99, I had to "add to cart"!

The special edition comes in two colors: Transparent Pink and Transparent Green; I chose the former.

This cube is identical to the regular MGC Beta, except in a pink tinged transparent plastic to show off the interior mechanism (e.g., the magnets, adjustment, interfaces, etc.). I saw it referred to somewhere as the "Aurora"; although I could not recall again where I saw it, I decided to call it that in my database, since it is such a poetic name. (Previously I referred to it as the "Pomegranate"!)

Maybe it's just placebo, but this cube feels slightly better than the regular Beta. It's probably the lack of stress brought about by the fact that there's nothing to solve! As such it's a great fidget.

I suppose you could order a YJ MGC sticker set from @thecubicleofficial to see if this cube really does feel better than the regular Beta... but why do that?
