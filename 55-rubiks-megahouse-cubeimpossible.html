Rubik's/Megahouse Cube Impossible

2621690 (2022)

Rubik's Impossible Cube. No, I didn't scramble it! The stickers are lenticular, so that most of them are one color when looked at from one angle, and another color from another angle. However, it turns out that it was carefully constructed so that it is not totally impossible. For example, the colors of the centers don't change. Also, the solved sides have the lenticular ridges going all in the same direction. And (according to the online solution manual) there are "anchor" tiles whose colors also do not change, which you can use to compare against the centers while solving. This means that the design of this random-looking object isn't random at all. It feels about the same as my other (non-magnetic) Rubik's cubes as well, which is much better than they used to be. So, very cool, but it might be a long time before I decide to try and scramble and solve it. Thanks to my sister @lechefl.ann for getting me this cube from Japan!
