Rubik's Crystal

6063214 (2021)

Rubik's Crystal 3×3 Cube. Bought it at the local toy store, which I was able to visit because of some suddenly-granted free time. Definitely is clear, with the inner workings revealed under translucent tiles. The turning feeling leaves something to be desired, but it's hefty; it's like the feeling of car tires as they skid on a bumpy road. Not magnetic. I don't think it can be tensioned (center caps don't lift off), but maybe lubrication will make it snappier. Also no instructions; which is only a little surprising, since the Rubik's Cube is such a puzzle institution by now. I think my "Clear Cubes" collection is complete. Well..., on the other hand, I am only a couple of cubes until I have 9 clear ones. That would be clearly superior.
