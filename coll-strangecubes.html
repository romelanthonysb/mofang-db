About this collection

This is a list of cubes that are off the beaten path, or are different enough from regular speedcubes or the other collection series.

In this collection are cubes in different sizes, slightly off-beat shapes, and mirror blocks (3×3 cubes whose faces are different axis distances, rather than colors).

Some notes

  • Even though these cubes will be different from the majority of my collection, they should still be 3×3 mofang (magic puzzle cubes) in mechanism.
  • This collection will probably have a low number of cubes, but I expect to collect the odd cube that catches my eye here and there.
