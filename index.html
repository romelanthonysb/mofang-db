Welcome!

Welcome to mofang-db, my effort to build an online display case for my cube collection.

Near the end of the pandemic, I picked up cubing as a hobby again, and in the beginning I only intended to find out how the new magnetic cubes felt like, which I've only heard about. But this led me to discover an entire world of different cube feels and mechanisms, which led me to amass a little more than 60 cubes.

I have been posting about my findings on Instagram, but there has been no good way to curate or collect them on that site. This is what led me to author this site you have landed on. I hope you enjoy browsing this site and taking a look at the cubes.

I know it's a throwback to have to say this, but please note that this website is under active construction! I concentrated on getting a few cubes up for display, so not all links might work. Please continue to check back for updates.

Some notes

  • This website is not meant to be an exhaustive list of all cubes ever made; the cubes herein represent those in my actual, physical collection. Read more about how I decided what to collect.
  • All of the information in this site, including the pictures and observations, were written and/or produced by me. Sometimes, especially in the beginning of my collection, I read from cubing forums and watched videos from lifestyle cubing channels on YouTube to glean information and make sense of what I was experiencing. You can look at my links page to see a list of cubing information sources that I have found especially useful.
  • If you'd like to reach out and talk about any of the cubes herein, please use my contact form.
  • None of the cubes are for sale. However, if you are interested in trading cubes, or if you have a lead on any of the cubes in my cube wishlist, please feel free to contact me.
