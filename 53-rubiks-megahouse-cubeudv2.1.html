Rubik's/Megahouse Cube UD v2.1

2626954 (2020)

Rubik's Cube Universal Design, V2.1. A tiled 3×3 Rubik's Cube with raised tactile patterns on the tiles to help distinguish them through feel. The colors also really stand out on their own. The cube turns marginally smoother than the original Rubik's cube, but is just as loud; it really wants to be seen, touched, and heard! Still have not had a chance to try to solve it blindfolded, or indeed, in a dark room, but since we still don't have power that opportunity might not be long in coming. A gift from my brother @arrbeeaiai from his travels in Japan; thank you!
